Chicken Fajitas Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

For the Fajitas:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 8-10 flour tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Optional Toppings:

  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Shredded cheese
  • Pico de gallo

Instructions

1. Marinating the Chicken:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Add the chicken strips to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for best results.

2. Cooking the Fajitas:

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large skillet or pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken strips to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until fully cooked and lightly browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  4. Combine: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir well to combine and heat through for another 2-3 minutes.

3. Serving the Fajitas:

  1. Warm the Tortillas: Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or until heated through.
  2. Assemble: Serve the chicken and vegetable mixture on the warm tortillas. Add your favourite toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese, and pico de gallo.
  3. Garnish: Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice.

4. Enjoy:

  1. Roll up the tortillas and enjoy your homemade chicken fajitas!
